About Us

Welcome to Millenial Mechanic, your number one source for all things automotive. We're dedicated to providing you with the very best of content, with an emphasis on manufacturer news, tips, and car reviews.

Founded in 2019 by Bushra & Elwad, Millenial Mechanic has come a long way from its beginnings in London. When we first started out, our passion for creating a space to congregate millennials like us who love everything cars, drove us to start our own blog.

We hope you enjoy our content as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,

Bushra & Elwad
